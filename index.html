About Me

Hello, and thank you for coming to my portfolio! My name is Neel Simpson. I'm working on making sure that the energy transition is socially just, ecologically sustainable, and economically efficient.

I earned a bachelor's degree in geology at the College of William and Mary, where I began learning about the relationship between natural resources, climate, and society. After working in environmental consulting, I decided to pursue a Master's in Development Engineering at UC Berkeley.

Through interdisciplinary research in earth sciences, development economics, and energy engineering, I hope to understand what's broken in our current energy systems and build a brighter tomorrow.
